About

PhD engineer. Serial founder.
Global award winner.

I build intelligent systems that ship — not just prototypes.

Currently Head of Data Acquisition at Network Nation Ltd, London — leading data acquisition strategy for a managed utility SaaS platform spanning electricity, gas, water, and heat metering.

Prior to this, I spent over six years as Senior Data Scientist & Systems Architect at Aquatronic / Stuart Turner Ltd (2019–2025), leading NPD in water hygiene and wastewater — STM32-based embedded controllers with MODBUS, BACnet, CAN, predictive AI, and TouchGFX HMI from schematic through to CE/UKCA-compliant production.

Co-founded Ayata Intelligence (ViShruti® AI eyewear — Intel India Best Hardware Startup, IoT/WT Innovation World Cup 2018) and Gravity Innovations (Samsung ARTIK Makers Against Drought winner, Top 10 globally). Previously led a team of 9 engineers at Mercedes-Benz R&D / Wipro on Level 5 autonomous driving and ADAS simulation.
